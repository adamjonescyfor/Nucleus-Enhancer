Good to know

  • Everything lives in Salesforce. This page is a faster window onto the same records — nothing is stored in the extension, and uninstalling it never touches templates or history.
  • Version history is automatic. Every content change is snapshotted, whether made here or directly in Salesforce. Status, team and date changes don’t create a new version.
  • Only Active templates reach analysts. Drafts, superseded and retired templates stay out of the insert menus. Everyone sees their own team’s templates plus Global, refreshed automatically (about every 20 minutes, or via Sync Now).
  • Teams and admin roles are managed in Salesforce — ask a Salesforce admin to add or move people.
