Welcome to Nucleus Enhancer 👋
  1. Connect Salesforce below — your team's official templates sync automatically.
  2. Insert templates — click the 📄 button or right-click in a Notes / Forensic Strategy box (Alt+T opens, Alt+Z undoes).

  4. View Templates shows your team's library with versions and history.
  5. Notes in list views are auto-formatted, columns are drag-reorderable, and there's more under Help & tips below.

            Help & tips
            ▾
        
        
      
            
    • Insert a template — click the 📄 button or right-click inside a Notes / Forensic Strategy box. Alt+T opens the menu, Alt+Z undoes an insert.
      • 
            
    • Quick-fill — right-click any date or time field to fill it with now; right-click Completed By or Sealed By to fill it with you.
      • 
            
    • Record navigation — Alt+← / → moves between process records in list order.
      • 
            
    • Columns — drag the column names above to reorder list-view tables; save layouts as presets.
      • 
            
    • Photographs — a "Download All Photographs" button appears on Uploaded Documents sections.
      • 
            
    • Notes — notes columns in list views are expanded and formatted automatically (toggle above).
      • 
            
    • Templates — your team's official templates sync automatically (about every 20 minutes); "Sync Now" forces it. "View Templates" shows versions and history.
      • 
            
    • Case report — export a sanitised disclosure report from any Forensic Case page.
